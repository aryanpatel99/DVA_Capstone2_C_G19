## 🚀 Projects

### 📦 [The Unplanned Basket](https://github.com/Arya756/DVA_Capstone2_C_G19)
> *Python · pandas · Tableau · Statistical Analysis — April 2026*

Quantified impulse-driven purchasing behavior across **13,000+ Blinkit SKUs** across 10 cities in India's quick-commerce ecosystem.

- Built end-to-end Python pipeline: cleaning → feature engineering → KPI computation
- Developed a composite impulse scoring model using pack size, perishability, and pricing signals
- Ran **Welch t-test, ANOVA, Mann-Whitney U** to validate pricing premiums statistically
- Key finding: ~21.6% of revenue is impulse-driven

### 🚗 [Road Accident Analytics Dashboard](https://github.com/aadit-vachher/section-c_g-11_travel-and-infrastructure)
> *Google Sheets · Statistics · Infrastructure Risk — February 2026*

Cleaned and analysed **~10,000 UK road accident records** to identify infrastructure risk patterns — delivering a decision-support dashboard for road safety planning.

- Processed records: deduplication, standardisation in Google Sheets
- Discovered peak risk: **October–November under low-light conditions**
- Found rural severity **1.16× higher** than urban; ~73% of casualties on single-lane roads
- Built interactive pivot-table dashboard with dynamic slicers
